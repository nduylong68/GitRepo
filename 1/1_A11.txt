Chuyen doi nhiet do. Dong lenh sau co nhiem vu doi tu do F 
(Fahrenheit) sang do C (Celsius). Lenh do co gi sai ?
double C = (F - 32) * (5 / 9);

Tra loi:
Chua khai bao F.
Trong truong hop da khai bao F va F mang kieu int thi de nhan duoc
gia tri C mang kieu double can phai ghi nhu sau :
double C = (double) (F - 32) * (5 / 9);
